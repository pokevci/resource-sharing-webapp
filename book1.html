Young Mongo

Douglas Stuart

Young Mongo is a compelling coming-of-age novel that delves into the life of a boy named Mongo, growing up in a world shaped by struggle, resilience, and the search for identity. Born into a harsh environment where he must navigate the complexities of family expectations, friendship, and love, Mongo finds himself constantly on the edge of survival and self-discovery. The story is set against a vividly portrayed urban landscape, capturing the gritty yet hopeful tone of Mongo’s journey. With lyrical prose and unflinching honesty, Young Mongo explores themes of belonging, courage, and the journey to find one’s place in a fractured world.

Im Glad My Mom Died

Janette McCurdy

I’m Glad My Mom Died by Jennette McCurdy is a raw, unflinching memoir that chronicles the author's turbulent relationship with her mother, as well as her struggles growing up in the spotlight. McCurdy, a former child actress best known for her role on the Nickelodeon show iCarly, opens up about her difficult childhood and the complex emotions surrounding her mother's death.

An Immense World

Ed Young

An Immense World by Ed Yong is a fascinating exploration into the vast and intricate sensory worlds that exist beyond human perception. In this thought-provoking book, Yong delves into the experiences of different animals, revealing how they interact with the world through senses that are often alien to us. From the way dogs perceive smells to how bats use echolocation, An Immense World challenges the human-centric view of reality, offering a deeper understanding of nature’s incredible diversity. With engaging storytelling and insightful science, Yong uncovers the hidden dimensions of life, showing that the world is far more immense and complex than we can imagine.
